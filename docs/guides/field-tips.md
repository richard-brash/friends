# Field Tips

These tips will help you use the Friend Helper app quickly and avoid common mistakes during outreach.

---

## Always Check the Location

Before recording an encounter, check the location shown at the top of the screen.

Example:

 City Hall Park

This ensures the request is recorded for the correct stop.

---

## Tap Names Instead of Typing

If the person has been seen at this location before, their name may appear under **Recently Seen Here**.

Tap the name instead of typing it.

This saves time and keeps names consistent.

---

## Use Item Modifiers When Needed

Some items require extra details.

Examples:

- Shoes (size 11)
- Underwear (men's)
- Jacket (large)

Enter these details in the **modifier** field when recording the request.

---

## Record Requests Immediately

It is best to record requests while you are talking with the person.

This helps prevent forgetting items later.

---

## Use “Repeat Last Request”

If multiple people need the same items, tap **Repeat Last Request** to quickly add them again.

You can adjust quantities before saving.

---

## Deliver All When Giving Items

When returning to a location with supplies:

1. Open the location
2. Look under **Needs Delivery**
3. Tap **Deliver All**

This records the delivery and updates the system.

---

## If Someone Is Not There

If the person is not at the location when you return:

Tap **Not Found** instead of Deliver.

This records the attempt so others know the delivery was tried.

---

## One Encounter Per Person

If someone requests multiple items, include them in a **single encounter** whenever possible.

Example:

Mike  
- socks (2)  
- blanket (1)

This keeps the request list organized.

---

## If You Make a Mistake

Don't worry.

Just record the correct information in the next encounter or let the coordinator know.

---

## When in Doubt

Focus on recording:

• **Who needs help**  
• **What they need**  
• **Where they were seen**

Everything else can be corrected later.

---

Thank you for helping make outreach more organized and effective.
